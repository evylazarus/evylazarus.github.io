About

Data analyst with experience using Excel/Tableau/SQL/Python to glean actionable insights from a wide variety of data sources. Excel at the full range of project life cycle, from initiatory business objectives, to exploratory data analysis, to model building, to story-telling with data.

My name is Evy, and I was born and raised in a world of visual arts. At age 16, I founded my own literature magazine, drew illustrations for it and designed it with Adobe Creative Suite. Now, whenever I took on designing project, I was always asked "are you an artist?", and I would proudly described myself as an visual artist who has keen eyes for individualized designs and visible solutions to meet client needs.

I continuously my exploration with art by studying sculpture and video art. From learning video-making softwares, I felt a strong desire to dig my hands into technology. After I started to work in marketing, I became a believer in data, social media, and their impact on the intersection of arts and technology. Now I have acquired the skills to extract, explore, and analyze data, I am confident that I can not only accomplish tasks with professionalism, but also come up with surprising solutions with my unique creativity.

I am also a conscious thinker seeking company that is attentive to diversity and sustainable development. Multi-lingual skills include Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean. Living with a corgi, who is a quiet and experienced office dog.
